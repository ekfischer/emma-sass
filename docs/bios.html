Member Bios

Harvey-Dale

Born on April 15, 1985, in Austin, Texas, Harvey-Dale is the charismatic lead singer of "Echo Harmony," a band known for their fusion of classic rock and modern vibes.

Harvey-Dale's musical journey started early, fueled by legends like Led Zeppelin and Jimi Hendrix. Forming "Echo Harmony" in the early 2000s, they gained fame with their 2010 debut album, "Echoes of the Soul."

With soulful vocals and powerful guitar riffs, Harvey-Dale's music has struck a chord with fans worldwide. He's not just a musician but a mental health advocate, using his platform to destigmatize issues like anxiety and depression in the music industry.

Harvey-Dale's dedication to music and his inspiring journey continue to captivate fans, making him an enduring figure in the world of rock and roll. His message: "Music heals, inspires, and connects us all."

The Cemements

Mady Rasco: The Bass Maestro

Mady Rasco, the band's talented bassist, hails from the vibrant streets of New Orleans. Growing up in the heart of jazz and blues, Mady's passion for music was inevitable. Her bass lines, both groovy and hypnotic, lay the foundation for "Echo Harmony's" unique sound. When not on stage, Mady enjoys exploring the world's culinary delights, often drawing inspiration from her travels to infuse new flavors into the band's music.

Michael Freyer: The Guitar Virtuoso

Michael Freyer, the band's guitar virtuoso, was born to make music. Raised in Nashville, Tennessee, he was exposed to country, blues, and rock at an early age. Michael's blistering solos and intricate melodies add layers of depth to the band's sonic tapestry. Outside of music, he's a die-hard vintage car enthusiast, often spending his downtime restoring classic automobiles.

Bradley Nix: The Rhythmic Dynamo

Hailing from the heart of Chicago's music scene, Bradley Nix is the rhythmic dynamo behind "Echo Harmony." As the band's drummer, his thundering beats and impeccable timing drive their songs to new heights. Bradley's love for percussion extends beyond the drum kit; he's an avid world percussion collector and enjoys experimenting with unique rhythms from diverse cultures. In his free time, you can find him exploring local music scenes and searching for hidden musical gems in the city's underground venues.
